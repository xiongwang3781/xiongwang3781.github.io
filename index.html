Research Focus

Hi, I’m Xiong Wang. I uncover the hidden stories of the Earth’s lithosphere by studying rocks, minerals, and their microstructures.

  • Combines laboratory deformation experiments and field investigations to explore mineral microstructures and rheology.
  • Links mineral physics experiments with geophysical data to reveal dynamic processes shaping the Earth’s lithosphere.
  • Expertise in MTEX for EBSD data analysis.
  • Leading AIMER (AI-assisted Microstructure Evolution in Rheology) for intelligent data analysis.

"Unveiling the ever-changing Earth beneath our feet through Lab, Data, and AI."
